Lemon Cake

Description

Fluffy lemon cake is the perfect dessert!

Ingredients

  • Lemon juice, 3 tablespoons
  • Flour, 2 cups
  • Milk, 1 cup
  • Melted butter, 1 stick
  • Eggs, 2
  • Sugar, 1/2 cup
  • Salt, 1 tablespoon
  • Vanilla, 1 tablespoon

Steps

  1. Beat eggs and add to flour.
  2. Add butter, milk, sugra, salt, vanilla, and lemon juice. Mix until smooth
  3. Pour into baking pan and bake at 370F for 1 hour.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

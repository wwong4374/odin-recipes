Delicious Steak Dinner

Description

This delicious steak recipe will surely satisfy your hungry guests!

Ingredients

  • Steak, 1 pound
  • Olive oil, 3 tablespoons
  • Butter, 1/2 stick
  • Garlic, 6 cloves, crushed
  • Salt, 2 tablespoons
  • Pepper, 1 tablespoon

Steps

  1. Season steak with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat olive oil in a frying pan, then place steak into pan. Sear on one side for 10 minutes.
  3. Flip the steak and sear on the other side for 10 minutes.
  4. Add butter and garlic to pan. Baste steak with the garlic butter.
  5. Remove from heat and let the steak rest for 15 minutes.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
